How to Get a Small Business Loan in Canada (2026 Guide)

A step-by-step guide to getting a small business loan in Canada — requirements, documents, loan types, and how to get approved in 24–48 hours for $5K–$800K.

The short answer
Most Canadian small businesses can get a business loan by matching with the right lender, submitting 3–6 months of bank statements, and completing a short application — with approvals often in 24–48 hours and funding from $5,000 to $800,000.

Getting a business loan in Canada is less about finding one perfect lender and more about matching your business to the lenders most likely to approve you. This guide walks through the process, the requirements, and how to improve your odds.

There are five steps: size the need, check the basic requirements, gather documents, get matched to lenders that fit your profile, and complete the application. You do not need perfect credit or years of history for every product.

What are the requirements for a small business loan in Canada?

Requirements vary by product, but lenders generally weigh four things: time in business (many alternative lenders fund from 6 months; banks want 2+ years), monthly revenue (a common floor is around $10,000/month), your credit profile, and your recent business bank statements.

What documents do you need to apply?

Having these ready is the fastest way to speed up approval: 3–6 months of business bank statements, government-issued ID, business registration details, basic financials, and a void cheque for funding.

How much can a small business borrow?

Through True North's lender network, Canadian small businesses can access $5,000 to $800,000. The amount you qualify for is driven mainly by your monthly revenue and time in business — most lenders cap funding at a multiple of your average monthly deposits.

How fast can you get approved and funded?

Approvals commonly come in 24–48 hours, with funding in as little as one business day.

Rule of thumb
The two biggest delays are missing bank statements and slow replies to verification. Have your statements ready and answer follow-ups the same day to fund fastest.

Does applying hurt your credit score?

Getting matched and seeing your options generally uses a soft check that does not affect your score. A hard credit inquiry may happen later, only when you move forward with a specific lender.

Frequently asked questions

How long does it take to get a business loan in Canada?
Many alternative lenders approve in 24–48 hours and fund within one business day, versus several weeks for a traditional bank.
Can I get a business loan with bad credit?
Yes for some products. Revenue-based options like merchant cash advances weigh your bank deposits more heavily than your credit score.
How much revenue do I need?
A common floor is around $10,000 per month, though it varies by product and the amount you request.
Do I need collateral?
Not always. Equipment financing uses the asset as collateral; term loans and cash advances are often unsecured but priced accordingly.
How new can my business be?
Many lenders fund businesses with as little as 6 months of operating history; banks typically want 2+ years.
